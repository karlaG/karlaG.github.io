WorkAround

A WorkAround to get you started.

WorkAround is a minimalist front-end framework built for DGMD E-27 at the Harvard Exntension School. It's fast, easy-to-use and frustration free. We're here to take all the fuss out of webdesign. With responsive, mobile-first designs and elegantly styled pre-built componenets, WorkAround can be used right out of the box and it will look good on any device. There is no need to know javascript as our platform is completely Sass based. Use our WorkAround to get you started. Browse through the documentation and an example webpage.
