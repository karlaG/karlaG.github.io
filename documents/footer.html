<footer>This is a footer.</footer>
This is a footer.

To give a webpage a footer, simply use the <footer> tag at the end of the body of the page and place whatever content you'd like to include inside the tag. By default, content is center-aligned with a top margin of 3em and 4em padding on the top and bottom of content, 2em on the right and left. To modify any of these styles, please see the Sass file _components.scss starting on line 50.
